About the Organisation

The Matahi Historical Society was formed by a group of Matahi residents in 1951 to promote the history of Matahi and its people.

Our main activities include:

  • Researching and studying the people and places associated with Matahi.
  • Preserving archival items of historical importance.
  • Highlighting important historical features in and around Matahi.
  • Supporting individual membersâ€™ historical projects.
  • Maintaining links with regional and national historical societies.

We also engage in the following activities:

  1. We meet four times a year and invite guest speakers.
  2. We arrange outings to places of historical interest.
  3. We publish a newsletter four times a year.
  4. We host photo collections online.
